Angela Tamayo

As a high school teacher, I’ve seen how overwhelming life’s transitions can be—especially when you’re figuring it out alone.
I know that struggle because I’ve lived it.
Through my own transformation journey, I learned how to overcome obstacles and grasp my dreams, and that’s why I became a certified life coach.
My mission is to guide others on their own path, offering support and clarity to help them achieve the future they’ve always envisioned.
Let’s turn “figuring it out” into thriving together.
